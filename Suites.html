Junior Suite

A Junior Suite is a single large room that features a separate seating area or living space, often without a full division between the living area and the bedroom. It’s slightly larger than a standard room and offers more comfort, typically suited for couples or business travelers who need extra space to relax or work.

Executive Suite

An Executive Suite is designed for business travelers, offering a separate bedroom and a dedicated work or meeting area. It often includes upgraded amenities like a larger desk, enhanced Wi-Fi, and access to business services. It's ideal for guests who need to conduct meetings or work during their stay.

Luxury Suite

A Luxury Suite offers high-end features and premium amenities. It typically has a large living area, separate bedroom(s), and may include features such as a private balcony, jacuzzi, or other luxurious furnishings. This suite is designed for guests seeking an upscale, lavish experience.


Penthouse Suite

Located on the top floor(s) of a hotel, a Penthouse Suite offers expansive views, a large outdoor terrace or balcony, and spacious interiors. It often includes multiple bedrooms, a large living space, and luxurious amenities like private pools or hot tubs. It's aimed at guests who desire both privacy and high-end features.
