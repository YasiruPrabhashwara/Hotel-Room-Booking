Executive Club Room

An upscale room type, providing access to an executive club or lounge, where guests can enjoy private check-in, complimentary refreshments, business facilities, and meeting spaces. It is typically chosen by business travelers who need both comfort and professional services.

Executive Family Room

A larger room designed for families who still want the executive-level experience. It typically offers extra beds or connecting rooms and retains the premium services and facilities, such as access to the executive lounge and enhanced in-room amenities.

Executive Deluxe Room

This room offers an upgraded version of the standard Executive Room, with larger living space and luxury amenities. Often located on higher floors with better views, the Executive Deluxe Room may include perks like premium toiletries, free Wi-Fi, and exclusive access to an executive lounge.
